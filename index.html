A bit of history

Africa is one of the richest lands in the world, containing gold, diamonds, sapphires, and various minerals. During the colonial period, European countries and industrialists exploited these resources without sharing the profits with Africa, leaving the populations in poverty.

After colonialism, dictatorships and corrupt governments took over, continuing exploitation without bringing positive changes. Cooperation also failed to improve the situation. As a result, the majority of African countries became poorer over time.

Civil wars, ethnic conflicts, and revolutions further destabilized these nations, leading to an inevitably bleak future. Climate change worsened the situation, prompting many people to seek a better life in Europe. Can they be blamed? Unfortunately, Europe often fails to offer a better future, and thousands of migrants perish in the Mediterranean trying to reach European shores. In response, European countries have closed their borders, but this is not a solution.

The Solution: Giving Africa what it deserves

In our view, the solution is to give Africa what it deserves by creating a future on the continent so that people have no reason to migrate to Europe. Is this a dream? Is it foolish to think this way? Time will tell.

Now, GEMs and the Consortium are here

A French businessman organized a consortium with several African countries to establish a fair diamond market. This consortium operates with the approval of the governments and military forces of these countries to ensure its protection.

To join this consortium as an investor, the minimum investment is $15 million (subject to application, compliance, and activity control of the candidate) and by cooptation only. Thanks to its relationships, ProfitLink negotiated an arrangement with the consortium and secured a place.

The profits generated by this activity are redistributed in a win-win relationship with all stakeholders (mines, miners, states, investors, the consortium, and all participants in this state-market). The consortium purchases diamonds from miners (under state control) at fair prices and respects the Kimberley Process (certification, ethics, and traceability of stones). Raw diamonds are then transported to Antwerp to the diamond exchange to be cut and polished. Once cut, diamonds are evaluated, certified, and sold to professionals.

ProfitLink, through its privileged position and MLM status, offers you the opportunity to benefit from this.
